S.H.A.P.E. Lab

Systems for Hybrid-Additive Process Engineering

The mission of the Systems for Hybrid-Additive Process Engineering Lab (SHAPE Lab) is to advance:

  1. 1.Additive Manufacturing: Design, Material Development and Biomedical Applications
  2. 2.Hybrid Manufacturing
  3. 3.Sand-Printing for Metal Casting
Research

Projects across the additive manufacturing landscape.

Research highlights connect students, tools, manufacturing equipment, and publications across additive manufacturing, hybrid manufacturing, binder jetting, biomedical AM, and sand printing for casting.

Sand Casting

Sand Casting

Advancing metal casting process via 3D sand printing

Increases casting design complexity, improves mechanical properties, and enables earlier defect identification.

Metal Casting

Printed molds, cores, and process design

Printed sand tooling supports complex thermal, geometric, and production-aware casting workflows.

Process Systems

Manufacturing-centered research environments

Students connect design, simulation, lab equipment, and inspection in complete research loops.

Hybrid Manufacturing

Hybrid Manufacturing

Additive Manufacturing + Mass Finishing Hybrid Manufacturing System

Improving metal AM surface finish and cost efficiency through post-processing and characterization.

DfAM

Design for Additive Manufacturing

Novel design rules, lattice structures, and optimization methods for process-aware parts.

Biomedical AM

Patient-specific and orthopedic additive manufacturing

High-fidelity biomedical geometries, biodegradable implants, and controlled scaffold structures.
